How We Work

Truth Index

Our proprietary Truth Index analyzes articles using multiple verification layers: cross-referencing with fact-checking databases, evaluating source credibility, detecting manipulated content, and measuring editorial bias.

A score closer to 100% means the article is backed by verified sources, contains minimal opinion, and has been corroborated by multiple independent outlets.

Our Evolution

2024

The Idea

The initial working version of NEWZON was developed with a focus on core functionality. This included a responsive frontend, multi-source news aggregation, and basic rule-based bias indicators using keyword frequency, sentiment scoring, and source-level categorization.

2025

Going Global

Machine learning and natural language processing techniques were introduced to enhance analysis accuracy. Articles were processed using linguistic features such as sentiment intensity, framing patterns, and cross-source similarity. Structured metadata and scoring logic were combined to form the first version of the Truth Index and Political Bias Indicator, with emphasis on interpretability over black-box predictions.

2026

The Future

Development is focused on improving model reliability, expanding datasets, and minimizing noise in bias detection. Visualization components are being refined to present scores more clearly and responsibly to users. This phase emphasizes continuous evaluation, transparency, and scalability for broader public use.
